Order Confirmation Email

An order confirmation email serves as the customer's safety net when shopping online. I created this project because many small businesses in my town do not follow this practice, and many do not realize how vital it is. The project will assist them in understanding that it is an excellent opportunity for businesses to start developing a customer relationship that will last beyond this transaction.
